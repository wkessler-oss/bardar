FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does bardar know how busy a bar is?

bardar blends anonymized live signals with on-the-ground check-ins and vibe reports from people inside, so the crowd levels you see refresh minute by minute, not last week's averages.

When and where does bardar launch?

bardar launches October 1, 2026 on iOS and Android, starting in major nightlife cities and rolling out to more shortly after. Get notified and we'll tell you the moment it's live near you.

Is bardar free to use?

Yes. Downloading bardar and seeing live crowd and vibe data near you is completely free. Always.

Do bars have to opt in?

No. bardar works for venues whether or not they participate, so you get an honest, unbiased read on where the night is actually happening.

How is my privacy handled?

Location and activity signals are anonymized and aggregated. We never share who you are or where you've been, bardar is about the crowd, not the individual.
